PROGRAM & ACTIVITY SUMMARY 2023

Which students Program Activity name about
All students Students' Hub The Students' Hub is a student-led initiative in a friendly and informal space with the basic premise of "advice from students for students". Ambassadors work informally with students sharing information about UOW services and programs and helping direct them to the support they need.
All students Study Skills Workshops Delivered by our Peer Academic Coaches, these workshops will set you up so that you can hit your study targets. Take a hands-on approach to success and gain the essential student tips and tricks. Whether it’s your first year at UOW or you’re a returning student, these workshops are for you.
First in family students First Gen Resources & events specifically designed to support students identifying as first in their immediate family to attend University.
Students enrolled in supported subjects Peer Assisted Learning (PAL) PAL Leaders facilitate group-based activities that incorporate important and challenging content with study tools, to help you successfully complete the subject.

ASSH: HAS120, PSYC123, PSYS150, SOC103
BAL: BUS121, ECON100, FIN111, LLB1100
EIS: CSIT115, ENGG252, MATH101/140, MATH141, MATH283
SMAH: CHEM104, MEDI100/111, SNUG103, SNUG202

International students International Student Success Course (ISS) Online series of modules covering key information about settling into Australia and navigating life, work, and study in a new environment. Includes tips about mental health & wellbeing, social welfare and safety, transport, study & work.
